# Feature Requirements Document (FRD 6): Ratings, Reputation & Trust System

**Product:** HealthNet Operating System (HOS)  
**FRD Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 2026  

**Classification:** *Trust Layer*  
Trust layer (infrastructure). Cross-cutting; enables trust across marketplace and suite. Nobody “buys” it; it underpins credibility of all products. See PRD §6.0a for platform-layer taxonomy.

---

## Objective
Build accountability and trust through contextual, moderated ratings and a weighted reputation model.

## Users
- Raters: Patients, professionals  
- Rated: Professionals, institutions, consultations, collaborations

## Functional Requirements
- Post-engagement ratings: 1–5 stars + mandatory comment (context-specific: consult, care plan, locum, referral).
- Weighted scoring: Time-decay, volume, rater credibility, category-specific (e.g., communication, outcome).
- Fraud/abuse detection: AI flagging (e.g., review bombing, fake accounts) + manual moderation queue.
- Display: Overall score, breakdown by category, recent reviews (paginated).

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Moderation SLA: <48 hours for flagged content.

## Regulatory & Compliance
- NDPC: No anonymous ratings; audit trail of all reviews.

## Constraints & Safety
- Dispute process: Rated entity can flag + provide evidence.
- No retaliation mechanism.

## Success Criteria
- ≥90% of engagements receive ratings.
- Fraudulent review detection rate >95%.
