# Feature Requirements Document (FRD 9): Security, Privacy & Governance

**Product:** HealthNet Operating System (HOS)  
**FRD Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 2026  

## Objective
Establish enterprise-grade trust, data protection, and governance to meet global healthcare standards.

## Users
- All platform users

## Functional Requirements
- Role-based access control (RBAC): Granular permissions (view/edit/approve).
- Patient-controlled consent: Per-consultation, per-feature, revocable at any time.
- Professional & institution validation: Thorough checks (document upload, registry API, manual review) to prevent fraud/quackery.
- Data export/portability: FHIR/JSON download.
- Immutable audit logs: All access, edits, consents.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Encryption: End-to-end for sensitive data; at-rest & in-transit.
- Breach response: 72-hour notification SLA.

## Regulatory & Compliance
- NDPC: DPIA, data residency (Nigeria), consent logging.
- MDCN: Credential integrity.
- GDPR/HIPAA-ready: For future expansion.

## Constraints & Safety
- Minimal data collection principle.
- No sharing without explicit consent.
