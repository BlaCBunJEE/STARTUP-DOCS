# Feature Requirements Document (FRD 8): AI Emergency Guidance & Clinical Safety Layer

**Product:** HealthNet Operating System (HOS)  
**FRD Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 2026  

**Classification:** *Safety Layer*  
Safety layer (infrastructure). Clinical safety infrastructure; differentiator, bundled and non-monetized initially. Educational and decision-support only. See PRD §6.0a for platform-layer taxonomy.

---

## Objective
Deliver context-aware, guideline-based emergency decision-support when life-threatening situations are indicated (text or voice input).

## Users
- Patients / bystanders

## Functional Requirements
- Intent detection: Keyword + NLP + voice prompts (inclusivity feature).
- Triage classification: Trauma, cardiac, obstetric, pediatric, respiratory, etc.
- Stepwise guidance: Follow regional/international protocols (e.g., WHO, Nigerian emergency guidelines).
- Escalation: “Call emergency services now” + connect to live professional if available. In Nigeria, it can automatically route you to the nearest hospital for immediate care.
- Resource-limited mode: Adapt advice for low-equipment settings.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Response time: <5 seconds.

## Regulatory & Compliance
- MDCN/NDPC: Educational only; strong disclaimers.

## Constraints & Safety
- Always prompt in-person/emergency care.
- Pre-launch: Clinical validation & approval by medical board.
- Continuous update: Quarterly clinical review.

## Success Criteria
- 100% disclaimer display rate.
- ≥90% user comprehension (post-use survey).
