# Feature Requirements Document (FRD 5): Care Plan Authoring & Subscription System

**Product:** HealthNet Operating System (HOS)  
**FRD Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 2026  

## Objective
Allow clinicians to create, publish, and manage structured, subscription-based care pathways for chronic, post-acute, preventive, and wellness management.

## Users
- Authors: Verified professionals  
- Subscribers: Patients (with consent)

## Functional Requirements
### Authoring Tools
- Drag-and-drop builder: Define steps, milestones, review intervals, tasks (e.g., medication adherence, exercise log), educational content.
- Eligibility rules: Age, condition (ICD-10), severity.
- Pricing & duration: One-time, monthly/quarterly subscription, tiered access.
- Red-flag logic: Automated alerts for missed check-ins, worsening symptoms.

### Patient Experience
- Enrollment: Review plan details → consent → payment → access portal.
- Progress tracking: Checklist, symptom diary, photo uploads, clinician notes.
- Check-ins: Scheduled video/async reviews, reminders via push/SMS.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Offline access: View/download plan; sync updates when online.

## Regulatory & Compliance
- MDCN: Clinician-authored only; informed consent per plan.
- NDPC: Granular consent revocation.

## Constraints & Safety
- Escalation required for red flags (notify clinician + emergency guidance).
- No substitution for in-person care when clinically indicated.

## Success Criteria
- ≥80% patient enrollment completion rate.
- Average plan adherence >70% at 30 days.
